# RSI-Momentum-Cross Primitive

## Profile
- **Signal Type**: Momentum (e.g., Trend, Momentum, Volatility, Regime)
- **Applicable Timeframes**: 1h, 4h
- **Applicable Assets**: All Liquid Assets
- **Orthogonality**: Measures velocity of price movement relative to range. (How this differs from other indicators)

## Logic
RSI crossing above 50 or 55.

## Failure Modes
- Overbought/Oversold extremes in strong trends (RSI stays high/low).

## Proven Compositions
- H3-B, H3-C, H3-D
