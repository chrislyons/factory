# The Agnostics

This was the initial high school band formed in late 2000.

**Context:** The group crystallized during the LJYO excursion period in Bowmanville, stemming from a high school orchestra/dinner setting.
**Timeline:** Formed by December 2000.
**Significance:** This iteration represented the initial, intense focus on songwriting outside of school requirements. It marked the beginning of the shared creative focus that defined the early friendship with Matt. This band served as the precursor to The Cheshire Smile.
