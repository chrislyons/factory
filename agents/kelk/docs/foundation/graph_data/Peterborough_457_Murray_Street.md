# Peterborough - 457 Murray Street

This was the first residence used during the post-dropout period.

**Details:**
*   **Address:** 457 Murray Street.
*   **Significance:** This location was part of the initial 'hippie-grifter' phase following the university dropout. The environment was characterized by a rejection of conventional life, including reliance on food banks and rejection of alcohol. Six people shared the cheap rental house (~$210/month per person), living as self-described counter-culture adherents.
