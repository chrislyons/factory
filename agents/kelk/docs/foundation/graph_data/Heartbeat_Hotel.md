# Heartbeat Hotel

This band emerged from the ashes of the previous iterations.

**Context:**
*   **First Generation (Mariposa):** This initial iteration mirrored the structure of the later Heartbeat Hotel.
*   **Second Generation (Heartbeat Hotel):** Formed in August 2008 after the recording of the "Transubstantiated Orphan Under a Harvest Moon" EP. This iteration incorporated Dave on lead guitar.
**Significance:** Represented a new, intense creative phase, validated by the enthusiastic response to the EP.

**Details:**
*   **Warehouse Era:** The group operated out of a 1500 sq ft factory warehouse starting in October 2007.
*   **Disbandment:** The three-piece effort (Chris, Matt, Andy) disbanded in March 2008.
*   **EP Recording:** The "Transubstantiated Orphan Under a Harvest Moon" EP was recorded in a single day in August 2008.
*   **Evolution:** The first-gen Mariposa mirrored the second-gen Heartbeat Hotel, and the second-gen Mariposa mirrored the first-gen Heartbeat Hotel, indicating a continuous evolution of the core creative unit.
