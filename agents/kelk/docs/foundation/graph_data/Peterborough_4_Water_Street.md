# Peterborough - 4 Water Street

This was the second residence used during the post-dropout period.

**Details:**
*   **Address:** 4 Water Street.
*   **Significance:** This location was part of the later phase of the intense, unstable creative energy following the university dropout. It was part of the collective lifestyle characterized by rejection of conventional norms. Together with 457 Murray Street, these two residences comprise the "Peterborough Slum" period.
