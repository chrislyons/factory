# Matt

Matt was a central figure in Chris's life between the ages of approximately 15 and 25. This friendship is documented as one of the longest and most significant in Chris's life, though it carries the weight of an 'enormous wound' due to its eventual dissolution.

## Early Connection & The Agnostics
Matt was first encountered during a Youth Orchestra (LJYO) excursion to Bowmanville in the summer of 2000, at a dinner hosted by his family. This initial meeting led to a fast friendship after the orchestra season began that fall. By December 2000, the core group formed The Agnostics, which included Chris, Matt, Julian, and Randy. Matt's early musical aptitude was noted, as he began Cello at age 4 and naturally fit the Bass role. The group was intensely focused on songwriting, heavily influenced by bands like The Strokes, and rehearsed weekly.

## University & Divergence
The initial band dissolved as university approached. While Chris remained connected to Matt through the orchestra, the friendship began to diverge as Chris entered his 'activism' years, leading to a period of lost contact.

## Reconnection & The Wild Years (Post-Dropout - 2006)
Following Chris's decision to drop out of university after Gwen's suicide attempt, the friendship re-established itself. Matt visited the Peterborough area, which presented a significant cultural contrast to his 'plain and docile' family life. Matt eventually integrated into the group dynamic after experimenting with acid. This integration caused a rift within the band, leading to the formation of The Cheshire Smile.

## Mariposa & Toronto (2006)
The group subsequently formed Mariposa. In September 2006, Matt's parents co-signed a bungalow in Toronto's High Park neighbourhood. This provided the group with seven-day-a-week rehearsal access. A formal co-writer relationship was established between Chris and Matt, though the writing dynamic often saw Matt producing the majority of the work.

## Warehouse & Heartbeat Hotel (2007 - 2008)
In October 2007, the group moved to a 1500 sq ft factory warehouse. After Dave was kicked out in Spring 2007, the core became Chris, Matt, and Andy. This effort disbanded in March 2008. In August 2008, the recording of the "Transubstantiated Orphan Under a Harvest Moon" EP occurred. Matt's enthusiastic reaction to this EP was noted as a key factor in the subsequent formation of Heartbeat Hotel.
