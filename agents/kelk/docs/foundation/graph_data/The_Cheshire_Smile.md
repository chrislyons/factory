# The Cheshire Smile

This band formed after the dissolution of The Agnostics.

**Context:** Formed during the period of transition following the initial band breakup.
**Significance:** This band was marked by the dynamic of Matt being perceived as an outsider within the group, leading to its eventual split. Michelle and Brandon left to have a child, which precipitated the formation of Mariposa with Matt formally joining the lineup.
