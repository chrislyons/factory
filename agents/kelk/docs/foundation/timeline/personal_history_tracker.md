## James & Cathy Conflict (Late 2011)

**Context:** James moved in with Chris at 187 Madison Avenue due to housing difficulties. He was struggling with heavy drinking and pills (OxyContin).

**Event:** A phone call between Chris and Cathy regarding Chris's drinking led to Chris making a comment comparing his situation to James's substance use. Cathy immediately called James, which Chris felt was a betrayal. He confronted Cathy later, but she refused to apologize. This event remains an undying source of anger and a feeling of being cheated.

**Significance:** This event is a major inflection point connecting the community loss (band failure) to the relational trauma (Cathy/James dynamic) and the core theme of betrayal.
