# Age 20-30: The Productive Years and Their Collapse

**Status: PARTIAL** — Geographic and work timeline now clear; emotional/relational gaps remain

## Overview

This decade divides into two distinct phases:

1. **2006-2010: Band Era** — Peterborough → Toronto with bandmates. Mariposa → Heartbeat Hotel. Zoomer career building. "Most productive period."
2. **2010-2016: Post-Band Era** — Solo at Madison Ave, then roommates. Left Zoomer 2013. Increasing instability (6 addresses in 6 years).

## The Carlaw Years (2007-2010)

The Carlaw apartment with Matt and Andy was the peak:

- **Mariposa** (Oct 2007 - Mar 2008): Broke up after difficult Ontario tour
- **Limbo** (Mar - Aug 2008): "Not even sure why we were still living together"
- **Heartbeat Hotel** (Aug 2008 - Sept 2010): First cassette, underground garage band, Toronto/Montreal focus
- **Side projects**: Blood Rexdale, Doldrums (both with Andy), Army Girls (Andy), Solid Gold (Matt)
- **Work**: Concert Engineer at Zoomer — 100+ broadcasts/year, ideaCity conferences

Matt and Chris operated as "the songwriters" on a Lennon-McCartney model. The internet hadn't blown everything open yet; they learned about music through the local record shop.

### The Dave Decision

Dave was kicked out when they moved to Carlaw (Oct 2007). Matt insisted on musical grounds ("not competent enough"). Chris pushed back — "cold and wrong" — but ultimately deferred. Andy sided with Matt. Chris had to deliver the news. Dave was crushed.

Chris's reflection: "I think it really shot the bottom out of our group. Dave was a very important piece of the puzzle and doing that to him really upset the math and the magic."

This echoes Randy being kicked from The Agnostics years earlier. Pattern: ejecting people for "musical reasons," regretting it later.

## Barcelona (October 2009)

Three-week trip arranged by Aunt Diane. Week one with Cathy and Dan in Barcelona. James was invited but declined.

**The incident:** On the last night in Barcelona, Cathy told Chris he was "like his Dad" in a rude way. Chris "completely lost his mind" and threatened to spend the night on the streets. This scared them.

Then two weeks solo through southern France and Paris — already destabilized. Spiraling in Paris, homesick, anxious.
