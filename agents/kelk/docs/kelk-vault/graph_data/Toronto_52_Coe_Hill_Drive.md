# Toronto Bungalow

This location served as the base for the Mariposa band in Toronto's High Park neighbourhood.

**Details:**
*   **Address:** 52 Coe Hill Drive. $1800 rent (split by 4 tenants). 3 bedrooms — Chris took the storage closet in the basement to make it work.
*   **Occupants:** Chris, Matt, Dave, Andy.
*   **Significance:** This period marked a significant step up in stability and access to rehearsal time, allowing the formal co-writer relationship between Chris and Matt to solidify. The group rehearsed in the basement seven days a week. Neighbours hated it.
