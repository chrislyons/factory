# Heartbeat Hotel EP Recording

This event marks the recording of the "Transbastardized Orphan Under a Harvest Moon" EP.

**Date:** August 2008.
**Location:** Factory Warehouse (388 Carlaw).
**Significance:** This recording was a pivotal moment. The EP was recorded in a single day, and Matt's enthusiastic reaction to the material validated the new creative direction, leading directly to the formalization of Heartbeat Hotel.
