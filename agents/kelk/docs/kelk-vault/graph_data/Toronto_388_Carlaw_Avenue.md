# Toronto Warehouse (Carlaw)

This location served as the base for the Heartbeat Hotel band in Toronto's Leslieville neighbourhood.

**Details:**
*   **Address:** 388 Carlaw Avenue. $2000 rent (split by 3 tenants). 1500 sq ft factory warehouse with 8' walls built by the tenants in raw industrial space, 10' ceilings — low privacy conditions.
*   **Occupants:** Chris, Matt, Andy (after Dave was kicked out in Spring 2007).
*   **Significance:** This was the space where the three-piece Mariposa disbanded in March 2008, and where the "Transubstantiated Orphan Under a Harvest Moon" EP was recorded in August 2008, leading to the formation of Heartbeat Hotel.
