# Age 7-14: Cobourg & La Jeunesse

**Status: PARTIAL** — Good coverage of music community, sparse on daily life

## Overview

The "golden period." Family moved to Cobourg, Ontario (small town) when Chris was 7. Both parents became directors in the local music organization **La Jeunesse**. Chris and James were immersed in choirs, orchestra, and piano lessons. Tight-knit community of ~8 families.

## La Jeunesse Ecosystem

**Structure:**
- **Girls' Choir** — The flagship, toured and won medals. Directed by Marie.
- **Apprentice Choir** — Feeder choir, directed by Cathy
- **SATB Choir** — Four-voice choir for older students
- **Youth Orchestra** — Directed by Michael

**Family Roles:**
- Michael: Directed Youth Orchestra, clarinet professor background
- Cathy: Directed Apprentice Choir
- Chris: Participated in choirs, orchestra, piano lessons, learned clarinet
- James: Same involvement, 3.5 years behind

**Community:**
- ~8 core families deeply involved
- Marie and Dan's family particularly close
- Kate and Connor (Marie & Dan's kids) = daily friends
- "If you had a kid, they were basically in the choir"

## The Feel

Chris describes this period as:
- "Felt like a star" in supportive, non-competitive environment
- "Very tight-knit community"
- "This was just life"
- Hanging out with Kate and Connor "pretty much every day after school"
